The Globe Bakery — live first

Premium organic · own the weekly habit

We build The Globe Bakery a premium organic website + online shop, and a weekly subscription box — the reliable loaf + pastry none of the local bakeries (Rock'N'Dough, Crwst, Loafley) offer. We match their craft and polish, then own the habit they've all left open: order online, collect or delivered. The content library builds from day one by shooting every bake. Local Facebook/Instagram + search marketing switches on once you're happy. First to go live.

Sancler Property

Real photos + video · sell on trust

We replace the CGI with real photography + video walkthroughs, fix the trust-killers on the current site today (it still says "Homes Ready 2023", the Site Plan page is blank, images load slowly), and drive buyers through the website itself — register-interest + brochure with automated follow-up. Per your steer, portals and paid ads are parked for now: website-only, organic-first, until you and Steve decide to open the taps.

Island House — the flagship

Sell the restoration · tell the story

We tell the story of Britain's medieval port as it's restored — a living journal, chapter by chapter — and set up the rigorous documentation a Grade II* listed building deserves. The history is done and fact-checked: the dig on your site uncovered the first medieval quay ever excavated in Wales — national history, and a grant + PR hook in one. The 20,000 photos get backed up and AI-sorted into ~50 clusters, not sorted by hand. New site + the story + the discovery/grants play.
