France — medieval icons (Steve's favourite)

How the French capture medieval heritage — the model that fits Island House best.

Living build★ The model

Guédelon

Burgundy, France

A team of master-builders is building a 13th-century castle from scratch, using only medieval methods and materials — since 1997. Visitors come to watch it rise.

Steal this for Island HouseThe process IS the story. Document our restoration live; make the craft visible; people follow it for months and years.

Carcassonne

Occitanie, France

Europe's largest medieval fortified city. Famously restored in the 19th century — and that act of restoration is now part of the story people come to see.
