Sancler Property · Website Architecture

How the website works

The blueprint for the Sancler site — built around one job: turn browsers into viewings with real photography and video, and capture every enquiry. A refresh of the current site, not a portal listing.

The rule for every page: real photos and video (never CGI), the homes shown at their best, and a register-interest capture never more than one click away. Website-only, organic-first — no portals or paid ads until Steve says so.

4 · How it's built (simple, lead-first)

Fast marketing site

Astro / Next on Vercel — quick, SEO-friendly, easy to update. Real photography/video led.

Lead capture + CRM

Register-interest forms → Airtable CRM; Make.com sends the brochure and the follow-up sequence.

Media (Cloudinary)

The real photos + walkthrough videos served fast and optimised.

Measure

GA4 + video-view tracking; a monthly report on enquiries and views.

Same-day trust fixes on the current site first (kill “Homes Ready 2023”, fix the blank Site Plan, speed the images) so we stop losing buyers while the refresh is built. No portals, no paid ads for now — organic-first.

5 · What we deliberately leave for later

  • Interactive plot picker + full 3D/virtual tours (once the core is live and filming's done).
  • Paid Facebook/Instagram lead ads + retargeting — parked pending Steve's OK.
  • Portals (Rightmove/Zoopla/OnTheMarket) — none for now.
