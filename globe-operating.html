Format & how the auto-pull will work
Columns: month, net_sales, cogs, labour, overheads — all ex-VAT in £ (commas, £ signs and a header row are ignored; tab-separated paste from Excel works too). month can be 2026-07 or Jul 2026. Overheads = everything below the line (electricity, insurance, rates, R&M, marketing, etc.). Net and the % gauges are worked out for you.

Later, automatic: the data loads through one function, loadMonths(). Today it returns the accounts baked in here plus anything pasted (kept in this browser). To pull live, point it at /api/finance (a serverless function reading the Monthly Accounts tab) — the panel below doesn't change.
