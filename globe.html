Who we are

A building with a hundred lives.

Hotel, coach station, butcher's, auction room — even a spot on screen in Dylan in New York and Keeping Faith. Today it's Laugharne's hidden gem: a bar, a kitchen and a secret garden, run on one belief — great food starts with care, for ingredients, for people, and for the land.

Grown, caught, reared, baked and poured in Laugharne — the courtyard behind Duncan Street, where the bread is made.
