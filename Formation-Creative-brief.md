# Creative Brief — Globe Bakery brand identity

**Prepared by:** Dominic Pullen, Pullen Marketing (on behalf of Stephen Kirkwood)
**For:** Formation Creative (or comparable Welsh place-based studio)
**Date:** July 2026

---

## The ask
Create an authentic, distinctive **brand identity** for a new artisan bakery — logo/marks, palette,
typography, packaging (kraft bands + labels), and the look-and-feel for a one-page website that bolts
onto the existing Globe site. It must feel **genuinely of this place and this maker** — not a generic
"premium" template.

## The business
- **Globe Bakery** — a new artisan bakery in **Laugharne (Talacharn), Carmarthenshire**, part of
  **The Globe** (bar · restaurant · bakery; company Globe Unlimited). Official name *Globe Bakery*,
  marketed as *artisan bakery*.
- Opening ~late July 2026. Trades **Tuesday–Saturday**; **collection only** (order-ahead + phone),
  with a weekly subscription box to follow.
- **Bread-first.** Real, organic, stone-ground bread with **no additives** (no sugar, no softeners) —
  Cotswold flour, honest ingredients. Pastries (croissants, Danish) are a secondary add-on.
- Range: traditional white, wholemeal, organic stone-ground, 8-grain multiseed, Cotswold Crunch,
  sourdough (large/small), soft rolls, baguettes; 400g/800g.

## Who it's for
Locals and visitors to Laugharne (a Dylan Thomas / heritage tourism town) who want proper bread —
including people who thought they'd given up on bread (health/gluten-sensitivity is a real story here).

## The soul (what makes it authentic)
It has to grow from a **real truth**, not a look. Three candidate directions (client to choose/blend):
1. **Rooted in Laugharne + Welsh** — bilingual (Popty'r Globe / Bara), estuary-and-township motifs,
   an old-enamel-sign roundel; ownable because no one else is *here*.
2. **The Bench (craft)** — identity built from the process: the **score-lines** cut into the dough as
   the motif, ink-stamped **kraft paper bands**, flour-dust textures, hand-made and imperfect.
3. **The Honest Loaf (people + health)** — plain-spoken, human, people-first ("flour, water, salt,
   time"), real testimonials, the bakers' hands and faces.

## Must-haves
- Works under / alongside **The Globe** brand (shared family feel).
- **Contained, tasteful** — Steve dislikes hero images that bleed off the screen; keep it composed.
- Photography-led (documentary, real — matching the Globe's own gallery feel), not stock.
- Practical packaging: a **kraft paper band** ("Globe Bakery") + per-bread **sticker labels**;
  micro-perforation bread bags; zip bags for pastries.
- Bilingual Welsh/English where it strengthens authenticity.

## Deliverables sought
Logo + variants · colour palette + type system · packaging (band + labels) · a simple brand guide ·
art direction for the one-page website (which Dominic's team can build in Shopify).

- Budget: TBC (please quote options/tiers).
- Timeline: bakery opening imminent — a phased approach is fine (core marks first, refine after).
- Assets: real bread photography being shot now; the Globe's existing brand for reference.

Your place-based Welsh craft-food work (e.g. Rye & Roses wood-fired bakery, Welsh Cottage Cakes,
