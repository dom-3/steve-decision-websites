A building with a hundred lives

Who we are.

Hotel, coach station, butcher's, auction room — even a spot on screen in the BBC's Dylan in New York and Keeping Faith. The Globe has worn many faces across its long life on Duncan Street.

Today it's Laugharne's hidden gem: a bar, a kitchen and a secret garden, run on one belief — great food starts with care, for ingredients, for people, and for the land.

— The Globe, Duncan Street, Laugharne
Out the back

The secret garden.

Tucked behind the bar, a walled garden that opens up when the weather's kind — long evenings, a drink in hand, and no passport required. Home to the Summer Sessions, live music and lazy afternoons.
