How this works — your first point of contact

One live place the whole engagement runs from. What that means in practice:

Your first point of contact. Jump on any time to see what's happening, steer direction, or reach me — updated live, every working day, so checking in never means chasing.
Decision-oriented. Everything is logged and tracked; anything that needs your call is put to you as a recommendation plus options — a quick pick, captured and dated on the spot.
A daily record, done automatically. Each day shows what was done and the window it was worked across — generated from the work itself, not written up after. It stays objective, and only real work is logged, never padding. The times are the span I work within on the day (often a long spread, not a fixed nine-to-five) — what matters is the output, dated and there to see.
Reach me two ways. Leave a note on any page, or email me directly — both come straight to me to action.

How we develop the process

The brief isn't the end — it's the start. Here's the loop we run from it.

1Direct. You read the brief and steer — approve it, or flag what to change.
2Decide. Each open call is put to you as a clear choice; your pick is logged here.
3Do. The work happens, captured once and used across compliance, story and marketing.
4Show. It lands on the work log and the business pages, dated and timestamped.
5Refine. We review, update the brief, and the process compounds across all three businesses.
