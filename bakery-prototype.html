Our Story

The baker, and the process

Bread this good doesn't come from a factory. It comes from early mornings, patient proving, and a baker who cares how every loaf turns out.

We use organic, stone-ground flour and nothing it doesn't need — no sugar, no softeners, no numbers. Just flour, water, salt, time and skill. You can taste the difference, and so can people who thought they'd given up on bread.

🌾 Organic stone-ground 🫙 Cotswold flour 🚫 No additives 🏴 Made in Laugharne
