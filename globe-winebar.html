A building with a hundred lives

Care — for ingredients, for people, for the land

The Globe has been a hotel, a coach station, a butcher's and more across its long life on Duncan Street — and it's appeared on screen in the BBC's Dylan in New York and Keeping Faith. Today it's Laugharne's hidden gem: a bar, a kitchen and a secret garden.

The kitchen starts where good food should — with care for the ingredients, the people, and the land that grows what we serve. By day, the same building becomes the bakery.

— The Globe, Duncan Street, Laugharne
