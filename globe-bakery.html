Allergen information

All bread baked here contains WHEAT (GLUTEN).

Our flour is milled in a plant where cross-contamination may occur — we cannot guarantee any loaf is free from other allergens.

Please ask a member of staff before you buy — we have the full ingredient list for every loaf.
