### Five-line finding

1. **The genuine Hughes-Garbett regatta-VP "Island House" styling is 21 AUGUST 1891, not 21 July 1893.** Carm Jrnl 21 Aug 1891 p.5, *"Laugharne Annual Regatta"* (WNO view/3794259/3794264), read in full: the regatta was held "on Monday last" in heavy rain; officers "President, Rev W. H. Harrison, B.A., vice-president Mr P. Ll. Hughes-Garbett, Island House," committee incl. E. W. H. Peel (Fern Hill). (F215-1, Verified-strong.)

2. **The 17 Apr 1891 p.5 "Island House vice-president" item is a DIFFERENT body — the Reading-Room & Institute AGM, not a regatta.** Carm Jrnl 17 Apr 1891 p.5, *"Laugharne. Reading Room"* (WNO view/3794097/3794102), read in full: the first annual general meeting of the Laugharne Reading-Room and Institute, held Thu 9 Apr 1891, Mr W. H. Saer presiding; "Captain J. N. Harrison, J.P., was re-elected president … and the vice-presidents are Mr Hughes-Garbett, Island House, and the Rev. J. M. Jones." So **two** separate 1891 items style Hughes-Garbett "Island House vice-president" — the Institute (17 Apr) and the regatta (21 Aug) — which is exactly how a snippet reader can misplace the regatta wording. (F215-2, Verified-strong.)

3. **At 21 July 1893, the "of Island House" regatta vice-president is R. W. PINNEY; Hughes-Garbett is only a committee member.** Carm Jrnl 21 Jul 1893 p.6, *"Laugharne. Regatta"* (WNO view/3796470/3796476), read in full — this **upgrades Doc 214's Partial read** (`reck9BMu9lcEfNRFl`) to a full object-level read. Regatta annual meeting, Town-hall, Rev J Thomas (vicar) in the chair; 1892 balance £3 5s; regatta fixed for Wed 9 Aug 1893. "Mr E W H Peel, of Fernhill, was elected president for the year, and Mr R W Pinney, of Island House, vice-president. The committee are The Portreeve, Capt Harrison, J.P., Messrs P Ll Hughes-Garbett, G R Brayshay, A Bolton, Lyon Falkener …" (F215-3, Verified-strong.)

4. **The whole 1893 field confirms it.** The 1893 AND-search (Hughes-Garbett AND Island House) returns exactly two Carm Jrnl items — 21 Jul 1893 p.6 (meeting) and 11 Aug 1893 p.3 (the event) — and in **both** the "Island House" vice-president is Pinney while Hughes-Garbett sits in committee. No 1893 item styles Hughes-Garbett "of Island House." (F215-4, Verified; the 11 Aug 1893 p.3 event report was read at object level by Doc 214.)

5. **Verdict on F212-3, and the residual tension dissolved.** Doc 212 §2's dated citation is a page/snippet co-occurrence conflation (the 21 Jul 1893 p.6 item does contain both terms, but the "of Island House vice-president" is Pinney). The true wording is the 21 Aug 1891 regatta report — locating it precisely also **corrects Doc 214's guess** that the conflation was with the 17 Apr 1891 Institute AGM. And the "21 Jul vs 11 Aug 1893" apparent contradiction is simply a **planning-meeting-vs-event** distinction: the 21 Jul meeting fixed the regatta for 9 Aug and the 11 Aug report is the event. (F215-5, F215-6, both Verified.)
