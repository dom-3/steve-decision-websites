How pre-order & collection works

No app, no faff. Tell us what you'd like, we bake it, and you pick it up from The Globe on Duncan Street. Collection only, to start.

Step 01
Reserve online
Use the form below to tell us what you'd like, how many, and which day you'll collect. That's it.
Step 02
We bake it
We bake in small batches so nothing's wasted. Your order is set aside with your name on it, fresh for the day.
Step 03
Collect from Duncan Street
Pop in to The Globe on your chosen day and collect your bread warm. Pay on collection.
