## Reconciliation flag — for the redesign AND for Karen
The live site repeats the **Mary Curtis (1880)** traditions as fact: Roman baths, leaden pipes, an Etruscan urn, Cromwell's cannon-ball, a rounded garden tower. Mererid's programme holds these at **tradition, not verified** (Doc 185). The redesign must frame them as *"what has long been said of the house"* — folklore, charming and honestly labelled — never as established fact. This honest-labelling is exactly the credibility upgrade the new research provides; it's also a heritage-publishing point for Karen before anything goes live.
